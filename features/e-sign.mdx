## Overview

The Drive AI includes built-in electronic signature capabilities so you can sign PDF documents directly in your workspace. E-signatures created with The Drive AI are legally binding and compliant with the ESIGN Act.

## How to Sign a Document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the PDF">
    Navigate to the PDF you want to sign in your workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select E-Sign">
    Click the **E-Sign** option from the file actions menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place Your Signature">
    Click where you want to place your signature on the document. You can draw, type, or upload your signature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Additional Fields">
    Optionally add your initials, date, or other fields to the document.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply and Save">
    Confirm your signature. The signed document is saved to your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signature Options

You can create your signature in three ways:

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draw** | Draw your signature using your mouse, trackpad, or touchscreen |
| **Type** | Type your name and select from signature-style fonts |
| **Upload** | Upload an image of your handwritten signature |

## Legal Compliance

The Drive AI's E-Sign feature is compliant with:

- **ESIGN Act** (Electronic Signatures in Global and National Commerce Act) — legally binding in the United States
- **UETA** (Uniform Electronic Transactions Act) — recognized across US states

<Info>
  Electronic signatures created through The Drive AI carry the same legal weight as handwritten signatures for most documents, including contracts, agreements, and forms.
</Info>

## What You Can Sign

- Contracts and agreements
- NDAs and legal documents
- Tax forms and applications
- HR documents and offer letters
- Invoices and purchase orders
- Consent forms

## Tips for E-Signing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Reuse Your Signature" icon="rotate">
    Once you create a signature, it is saved for future use. You do not need to redraw it each time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Review Before Signing" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Always review the full document before applying your signature. Use Chat Mode to ask questions about the document content if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Combine with PDF Tools" icon="file-pdf">
    Use PDF tools to fill out forms before signing. For example, fill out a contract with Agent Mode, then apply your signature.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
