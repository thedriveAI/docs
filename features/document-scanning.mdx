## Overview

The Drive AI mobile app turns your phone into a document scanner. Capture physical documents with your camera and instantly convert them to searchable PDFs in your workspace—complete with OCR text extraction so you can search, query, and organize scanned documents just like any other file.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Mobile App">
    Open The Drive AI app on your iOS or Android device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Scan">
    Tap the scan button to activate your camera.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture the Document">
    Point your camera at the document. The app automatically detects document edges and captures a clean image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Save">
    Review the scan, adjust if needed, and save it to your workspace as a searchable PDF.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

### Automatic Edge Detection

The scanner automatically detects the edges of your document, correcting for angle and perspective. You get a clean, straight scan even if you capture it at an angle.

### OCR Text Extraction

Every scanned document is processed with OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to extract all text content. This means your scanned documents are:

- **Fully searchable** — find text within scanned documents using search or Chat Mode
- **AI-ready** — ask questions about scanned document content
- **Auto-organized** — the AI reads the content to organize scanned files into the right folders

### Multi-Page Scanning

Scan multiple pages in sequence to create a single multi-page PDF. This is perfect for:

- Multi-page contracts
- Receipts and expense reports
- Meeting notes and handwritten documents
- Forms and applications

### Automatic Upload

Scanned documents are automatically uploaded to your Drive AI workspace. Once uploaded, they go through the same AI processing as any other file—text extraction, organization, and indexing for search.

## Tips for Better Scans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Good Lighting" icon="sun">
    Scan in well-lit areas for the best results. Avoid shadows falling across the document.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Flat Surface" icon="table">
    Place documents on a flat, contrasting surface. A dark document on a light surface (or vice versa) helps with edge detection.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Steady Hand" icon="hand">
    Hold your phone steady while capturing. The app works quickly, but a stable hand produces sharper results.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multiple Pages" icon="copy">
    For multi-page documents, scan all pages in one session to create a single PDF rather than separate files.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
