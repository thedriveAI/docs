## Overview

The Drive AI integrates with Gmail and Outlook to automatically import email attachments to your workspace. Stop manually downloading and organizing attachments—let The Drive AI handle it automatically.

<Note>

</Note>

## Why Connect Your Email?

Email attachments are one of the biggest sources of document chaos:

- Contracts buried in threads
- Invoices scattered across months of emails
- Reports you can never find when you need them
- Important documents lost in your inbox

With The Drive AI email integration:

- **Attachments are automatically imported** to your workspace
- **AI organizes them** into logical folders
- **Find anything instantly** with natural language search
- **Never lose an important document** again

## Connecting Gmail

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Settings** > **Integrations** > **Email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Gmail">
    Click **Connect Gmail**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Sign in to your Google account and grant The Drive AI permission to access your email attachments.

    <Note>
      The Drive AI only accesses attachments. We do not read your email content or store your messages.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Choose your import preferences:
    - Which folders/labels to monitor
    - Auto-organization settings
    - Import frequency
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Importing">
    Click **Enable** to start automatic attachment imports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How It Works

### Automatic Import

Once connected, The Drive AI monitors your inbox for new attachments:

1. **New email arrives** with an attachment
2. **The Drive AI detects** the attachment
3. **Attachment is imported** to your workspace
4. **AI organizes** the file into the appropriate folder

All of this happens automatically—you don't need to do anything.

### What Gets Imported

By default, The Drive AI imports common document types:

- **Documents**: PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT
- **Spreadsheets**: XLSX, XLS, CSV
- **Presentations**: PPTX, PPT
- **Images**: PNG, JPG, JPEG (Premium)

You can customize which file types to import in your settings.

### Organization

Imported attachments are organized based on:

| Factor | Example |
|--------|---------|
| **Sender** | Files from "client@company.com" → Client folder |
| **Content** | Invoice detected → Invoices folder |
| **Subject** | "Q4 Report" → Reports folder |
| **Date** | Organized by month or quarter |

## Configuration Options

### Folder Selection

Choose which email folders to monitor:

- **All folders**: Import attachments from anywhere
- **Inbox only**: Only import from your main inbox
- **Specific folders**: Select individual folders/labels

### File Type Filters

Control which file types are imported:

- Include or exclude specific extensions
- Set minimum/maximum file sizes
- Skip duplicate files

### Import Frequency

Choose how often The Drive AI checks for new attachments:

- **Real-time**: Import immediately as emails arrive
- **Hourly**: Check every hour
- **Daily**: Check once per day

### Destination Folder

Choose where imported attachments go:

- **Workspace root**: Auto-organization sorts them
- **Specific folder**: All attachments go to one place (e.g., "Email Attachments")
- **Organized by sender**: Separate folders for each sender

## Viewing Imported Attachments

### Finding Attachments

Use Chat Mode to find imported email attachments:

```
Find the invoice that came from accounting@company.com last week
```

```
Show me all attachments from emails about the Johnson project
```

### Email Metadata

Imported attachments retain metadata about their source:

- Sender email address
- Email subject
- Date received
- Original filename

## Managing Your Email Connection

### Pausing Import

To temporarily stop importing without disconnecting:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Integrations** > **Email**
2. Toggle **Pause Import** for the desired account

### Disconnecting

To completely disconnect your email:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Integrations** > **Email**
2. Click **Disconnect** next to the email account
3. Confirm the disconnection

<Warning>
  Disconnecting does not delete already-imported attachments. It only stops future imports.
</Warning>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What data do you access?" icon="eye">
    The Drive AI only accesses email attachments. We do not read, store, or process the text content of your emails. Only attachment files are imported to your workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?" icon="shield-halved">
    Yes. All data transfers are encrypted. Your email credentials are handled through secure OAuth—we never see or store your password.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I revoke access?" icon="key">
    Yes. You can disconnect The Drive AI from your email account at any time. You can also revoke access from your Google or Microsoft account settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Attachments Not Importing" icon="file-circle-xmark">
    - Check that the email integration is enabled (not paused)
    - Verify the attachment file type is in your include list
    - Ensure you have sufficient storage space
    - Check that the email folder is being monitored
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Missing Old Attachments" icon="clock-rotate-left">
    By default, only new attachments (after connection) are imported. To import historical attachments, use the **Import History** option in settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Duplicate Files" icon="copy">
    Enable **Skip Duplicates** in your settings to prevent the same attachment from being imported multiple times.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
