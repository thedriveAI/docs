## What is The Drive AI?

[The Drive AI](https://thedrive.ai) is an AI-powered workspace where all file operations—creating, sharing, organizing, and managing files—can be done using plain English. Think of it as ChatGPT for your files.

Instead of navigating folders, copying content, or manually organizing documents, you simply describe what you want to accomplish. The Drive AI figures out the steps and executes them automatically.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your Files">
    Drag and drop files into your workspace. No need to worry about folder structures upfront.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Analyzes Content">
    The Drive AI reads and understands the content, context, and relationships between your files.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Organization">
    Files are placed in the right folders instantly, or you can trigger organization manually with `Command+K`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Interact Naturally">
    Use Chat Mode to ask questions or Agent Mode to perform actions—all through natural language.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How Is This Different From Traditional Tools?

Traditional file management tools give you buttons, menus, and folders—and expect you to do all the work yourself. You manually create folders, drag files around, rename them one by one, and spend hours organizing.

**The Drive AI is different:**

| Traditional Tools | The Drive AI |
|-------------------|--------------|
| Manual folder creation | AI creates folders based on content |
| One file at a time | Bulk operations via natural language |
| Click-heavy workflows | Describe what you want in plain English |
| You organize | AI organizes for you |
| Search through folders | Ask questions, get answers |
