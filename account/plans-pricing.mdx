## Overview

The Drive AI offers flexible pricing for individuals and teams. Everyone starts with a **Free** plan and can upgrade to **Premium** or **Max** for more AI power, storage, and integrations. Teams can create **Team Workspaces** for collaborative file management.

## Workspaces

The Drive AI uses workspaces to organize your files. There are two types:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Personal Workspace" icon="user">
    **For individuals**

    - One per account (created automatically)
    - Single user only
    - Free, Premium, or Max plan
    - Cannot be deleted or recreated
  </Card>
  <Card title="Team Workspace" icon="users">
    **For collaboration**

    - Up to 50 team members
    - Different access levels
    - Single billing account (managed by owner)
    - Create multiple team workspaces
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Switching Workspaces

To switch between workspaces or create a new team workspace:

1. Click the **workspace name** at the top of the left sidebar
2. A dropdown shows all your workspaces
3. Select a workspace to switch, or click **Create Workspace** to create a new team workspace

## Team Workspace Pricing

For teams that need to collaborate on files together.

<Card title="Team Workspace" icon="users-gear">
  **$500/month** per workspace

  <Note>Includes a **1-month free trial** for each new team workspace</Note>
</Card>

### What's Included

- **Up to 50 team members** per workspace
- **Role-based access levels** (Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer)
- **Centralized billing** managed by workspace owner
- **All Max features** for every team member
- **Shared file organization** across the team
- **Team-wide AI capabilities**
- **Multiple integrations** in a shared workspace

### Creating a Team Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Switcher">
    Click the workspace name at the top of the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Workspace">
    Click **Create Workspace** at the bottom of the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Workspace">
    Enter a name for your team workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Free Trial">
    Your 1-month free trial begins immediately. Credit card details required to start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite Team Members">
    Add team members and assign access levels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### After the Free Trial

After your 1-month free trial ends:
- You will be charged.
- Billing is **$500/month** per team workspace
- The workspace owner manages billing
- Cancel anytime—workspace remains accessible until the end of the billing period
- Workspace will be in read-only mode
- Delete workspace to delete all files.

<Info>
  You can create multiple team workspaces. Each workspace has its own 1-month free trial and separate $500/month billing.
</Info>

## How to Upgrade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Log in to your The Drive AI account at [thedrive.ai](https://thedrive.ai).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    Click on your profile and navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a Plan">
    Choose **Premium** or **Max**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Enter your payment information and confirm the subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using Your Plan">
    Your account is upgraded immediately. All features for your plan are now available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Your Subscription

### View Current Plan

Go to **Settings** > **Billing** to see:
- Your current plan
- Billing cycle dates
- Storage usage
- AI step usage

### Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation

<Info>
  When you cancel, you keep access until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.
</Info>

### What Happens When You Downgrade?

If you cancel or downgrade:

- Your files remain in your workspace
- Files over your plan's storage limit become read-only until you delete some
- Media files (images, audio, video) become inaccessible on the Free plan
- AI steps and auto-organization revert to the lower plan's limits
- Integrations exclusive to your previous plan are paused
